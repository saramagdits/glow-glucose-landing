1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is generated through your use of the App. All data is stored locally on your device unless otherwise specified.

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use the App, you may choose to provide:

- Name: Your first name (optional, used for personalization)
- Pregnancy/Birthing Stage: Whether you are pregnant, postpartum, or other
- Due Date: Your expected due date (if pregnant)

1.2 Health and Fitness Data

The App collects and stores the following health-related information locally on your device:

- Blood Glucose Readings: Fasting, pre-meal, post-meal, and general blood glucose values (in mg/dL) along with timestamps
- Meal Information: Meal types (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack), dates, and times
- Nutritional Data: Calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fat content of meals
- Food/Ingredient Information: Names, quantities, and nutritional details of foods you log

1.3 User Content

- Meal Photos: Photos you take or select for meal logging. These photos are:
  - Stored locally on your device
  - Temporarily processed by our nutrition analysis service to identify foods (see Section 4)
  - Not permanently stored on our servers

1.4 App Settings and Preferences

- Blood Glucose Targets: Your personalized glucose threshold settings
- Reminder Preferences: Your notification and reminder settings
- Display Preferences: Chart height and other visual settings

1.5 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect:

- Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for app compatibility)
- App Usage Data: Feature interactions for improving app functionality
- Crash and Performance Data: Error logs and performance metrics to improve app stability

1.6 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to our service:

- Subscription Status: Whether you have an active subscription
- Transaction Identifiers: Purchase receipts processed through Apple's App Store
- Subscription Period: Start and expiration dates of your subscription

Note: We do not collect or store your payment card details. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 App Functionality

- To enable meal logging and blood glucose tracking features
- To analyze meal photos and identify food items for nutritional information
- To calculate and display nutritional data for your meals
- To send reminder notifications for blood glucose testing (if enabled)
- To personalize your experience based on your settings

2.2 Subscription Management

- To verify your subscription status and provide access to premium features
- To process subscription purchases and renewals through Apple's App Store

2.3 App Improvement

- To identify and fix bugs and technical issues
- To improve app performance and user experience
- To develop new features based on usage patterns

2.4 Communication

- To send you reminders you have enabled (glucose testing reminders)
- To notify you of important changes to the App or this Privacy Policy

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

3.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database technology. This includes:

- All meal and ingredient records
- All blood glucose readings
- Your personal settings and preferences

This means your health data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers for storage.

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- Local data encryption on your device (provided by iOS)
- Secure HTTPS connections for any data transmitted to third-party services
- No server-side storage of your health data

3.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Maintaining the security of your device
- Using device passcodes or biometric locks
- Keeping your device's operating system updated

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Passio Nutrition AI

- Purpose: Food recognition and nutritional analysis
- Data Shared: Meal photos (temporarily processed for food identification)
- Data Received: Food names, nutritional information
- Privacy Policy: https://www.passio.ai/privacy-policy

When you take a photo for meal logging, the image is sent to Passio's servers through our secure proxy for food recognition. The image is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored by Passio or us.

4.2 RevenueCat

- Purpose: Subscription and purchase management
- Data Shared: Anonymous user identifier, purchase transactions
- Data Received: Subscription status, entitlement information
- Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

RevenueCat helps us manage subscriptions and does not have access to your health data.

4.3 Apple App Store

- Purpose: Payment processing and app distribution
- Data Shared: Purchase transactions (handled by Apple)
- Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

All payment processing is handled directly by Apple. We never receive or store your payment card information.

4.4 Expo/React Native

- Purpose: App framework and notifications
- Data Shared: Push notification tokens (if notifications enabled)
- Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or health data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- Service Providers: With third-party services described in Section 4, solely to provide app functionality

- Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Glow Glucose, our users, or others

5.3 Aggregated Data

We may use aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you for research, analytics, or improving our services. This data contains no personal identifiers.

6. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

6.1 Access and Control

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have direct control over it:

- View Your Data: Access all your meals, glucose readings, and settings within the App
- Edit Your Data: Modify any information you have entered
- Delete Your Data: Delete individual records or all data by uninstalling the App

6.2 Notification Preferences

You can manage notification permissions through your device's Settings app at any time.

6.3 Subscription Management

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings:
1. Open the Settings app on your device
2. Tap your name, then tap Subscriptions
3. Select Glow Glucose to manage or cancel

6.4 California Residents (CCPA Rights)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 11.

6.5 European Residents (GDPR Rights)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate personal data
- Request erasure of your personal data
- Restrict processing of your personal data
- Data portability
- Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 11.

7. DATA RETENTION

7.1 Local Data

Data stored on your device remains there until you delete it or uninstall the App. We do not have access to data stored locally on your device.

7.2 Third-Party Data

- Meal Photos: Processed in real-time by Passio and not permanently stored
- Subscription Data: Retained by RevenueCat as long as you have an active subscription, subject to their privacy policy
- Purchase Records: Retained by Apple according to their policies

7.3 After Uninstallation

When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.
