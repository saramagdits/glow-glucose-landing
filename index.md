# Glow Glucose

A calm space for your gestational diabetes journey.

## About

Glow Glucose is designed to support women during pregnancy with gentle tracking, AI-powered meal insights, and beautiful visualizations of your health data.

## Features

### AI Meal Logging

Simply snap a photo of your meal and our AI instantly identifies ingredients and calculates carb counts. No more tedious manual entry or guesswork.

### Blood Sugar Logging

Quick and easy glucose logging with smart reminders. Track fasting, pre-meal, and post-meal readings to stay on top of your targets.

### Trends & Charts

Beautiful visualizations help you understand your patterns. See daily, weekly, and monthly trends with averages and insights at a glance.
